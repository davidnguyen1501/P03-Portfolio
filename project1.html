3D ANIMATION PROJECT

Overview

This project was worked on by a team of 3 and was presented as a final project in an Animation course (IAT 343) offered at Simon Fraser University, the project requires us to create a 3-5 minutes long animation with a unique and well planned plot with convincing narrative to push forward in Autodesk Maya, we have to divide our tasks individually and each have to take part in making the entire animation We had around 8 weeks to plan out the project, brainstorming and drawing the storyboard, creating individual 3D models in Autodesk Maya and finally put them together in a completed animation video

Process

Ideation Process

To start off with the project, we had to brainstorm some ideas for the plot and create a storyboard.One of my teamates suggested doing an animation featuring bees so we decided to work on a plot based on it. I was responsible in creating rough sketches and planning out the camera angle used in the final animation.

Modeling

We used AutoDesk Maya to model our Bees and texturing it using Photoshop, I was responsible for making the main Character, creating the shape and skeleton to turn him into a lively being

Animating

Animation was for sure the toughest part, it took us a lot of time and effort to get everything done right, I was responsible for animating the begining scene of the movie
